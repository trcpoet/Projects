Company History:

Special Coffee Connoisseur was founded in 2001 in the busy city of Arlington, Texas.
It was founded on the basis of providing customers with cheap and good coffee and coffee beans.
Founded by Tahsin Rahman Chowdhury, we aspire to provide the best coffe beans sources from the
most ethical farmers. SCC begun as a small project for Chowdhury but quickly took over as
people from all over the world started to encourage his passion

Mission Statement:
"Creating extraordinary coffee experiences." We strive to live by this statement.
Everyday when we open up shop we strive to provide the best customer experience in
the DFW area and ALL AROUND THE WORLD. The baristas at SCC are always there to help
you and guide you through your coffe buying journey.
